How to make Chai

Ingredients :

  • Water
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Tea Leaves
  • Ginger

Recipe :

  1. Boil Water
  2. Add Milk
  3. Add Sugar
  4. Add Tea Leaves
  5. Add Ginger
  6. Boil for 5 minutes
  7. Strain and Serve

What is Chai and Why It is so Popular in India?

Chai
=> Masala chai is an Indian beverage popular throughout South Asia. Chai is made by brewing black tea in milk and water and then sweetening with sugar. Adding aromatic herbs and spices creates masala chai, although chai is often prepared unspiced.
Why Chai is so Popular in India?
=> Chai is popular in India because it is a part of the culture and the way of life. It is a drink that is served to guests, it is served to family members, it is served to friends, it is served to strangers, it is served to everyone. It is a drink that is served to everyone.
